# Project brief

## What this product is
**Agent Control Tower** (codebase under `agent-control-tower/`) is an enterprise Agent Access + Audit Layer: the control plane for company AI agents.

## Target user
- Internal AI platform teams
- Engineering platform / DevOps / ITOps
- Security and governance teams
- Operations teams deploying internal AI agents

## Current product thesis
Trust is the blocker for agent adoption: who is this agent, what can it access, what did it do, who approved risky actions, how do we investigate? This product answers those questions via registry, policy, approval workflow, and audit/trace.

## Non-goals (MVP)
- Full external marketplace
- On-chain reputation
- Production-grade billing
- Autonomous deployments
